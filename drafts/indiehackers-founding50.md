# Open Primitive — 17 federal data APIs for AI agents. Founding 50: $19/mo forever.

We built Open Primitive to solve a specific problem: US government data is public but unusable. PDFs, broken XML feeds, inconsistent schemas, no versioning. If you've tried to pull structured data from FDA, EPA, CMS, or BTS, you know.

Open Primitive is a single API layer over 17 federal data domains from 11 agencies. Drug adverse events. Hospital quality scores. Vehicle recalls. Drinking water violations. Food safety alerts. Flight delay patterns. All of it normalized, typed, and ready for programmatic consumption.

## Who this is for

Developers building AI agents that need grounded federal data. Compliance tools. Health apps. Civic tech. Anyone who needs a reliable machine-readable interface to government information without scraping it themselves.
